Start.Stay.Grow. is a series of ongoing initiatives that work to bridge the gap between Philadelphia’s young talent and the tech startup community.


This program is encouraging young talent to START their career in the Philly startup scene by increasing awareness of opportunities and the startup community; providing opportunities for young talent to STAY in Philly by creating a network of recent/soon-to-be grads and tech startups with talent needs; and empowering founders to GROW their businesses in Philly by connecting support systems such as mentors, funding, and additional resources in the city.

  • What is the PSL STARTup Fellowship

    The PSL STARTup Fellowship is part of Philly Startup Leaders’ START. STAY. GROW. Program, aimed at bridging the gap between Philadelphia’s Young Talent and the Tech Startup Community. The fellowship focuses on encouraging young talent to START their career in the Philly startup scene by increasing awareness of opportunities and the startup community.

  • Who is Eligible to Become a Fellow

    We are seeking fellows to participate in a 10-week Fellowship (June 5th – August 18th) during which, fellows will work with one of our Partner Startups 3-4 days / week and PSL will lead workforce development programming 1 day / week. Both undergrads & master students who are available to participate in the entire 10-week fellowship are eligible.

  • What Roles are Available

    Open positions vary by startup, but opportunities exist for marketing/sales, business development, design, logistics/operations, and web/mobile development.

  • How do I Apply

    Any interested eligible candidate can fill out an application here. Applications will open on February 9th and are due by 11:59 PM EST on March 15th.
