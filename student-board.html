- About -

The PSL Student Board is a pan-Philadelphia organization dedicated to connecting students to entrepreneurship-oriented opportunities, especially those within the Philly startup ecosystem. Led by student representatives from schools all across the city, the Board and its members provide resources like online guides and podcasts, hosts events like [INSERT FUTURE EVENTS], and helps students find the right path to help develop their interests in various aspects of entrepreneurship and startups. Our mission is to make it easier for students to explore startups, one of the most exciting opportunities of the 21st century job market, and start, grow, and build a company or a career in one of the best cities in the country, Philadelphia!

JON GULARDO

My Name is Jon Gulardo and I am a senior in Drexel’s Lebow College of Business and Close School of Entrepreneurship. Deciding to plug in to the Philadelphia startup community has by far been one of the best decisions I have made since coming to school 4 years ago. I owe my excitement about this city to the support and resources offered by PSL; I have met, learned from, and made friends with a number of talented students, community leaders, entrepreneurs (both accomplished and aspiring), and start-up builders alike. However, much of this was due to stumbling upon a last second email for PSL’s startup boot camp late last year, which accelerated my involvement within the ecosystem. As a student, it is easy to oversee even a vibrant community when it's outside of the immediate one on campus, but I am now very excited to play a helping hand in sharing what this amazing city has to offer. Philadelphia is a great place to be not just as a student, but for anyone looking for support, guidance and success after school. The PSL Student board is here as a resource to show what is possible here!
